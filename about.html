About Me

Founder of SHAN Academy of Quantum Simulation & Materials

Zeshan Ahmed

I am an MPhil scholar in Physics, specializing in materials science with a strong focus on quantum simulations. My expertise lies in applying Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Molecular Dynamics (MD) to investigate the electronic, optical, thermal, and adsorption properties of advanced materials.

Through SHAN Academy, I provide training and resources to help students learn tools such as CASTEP, DMol³, Forcite, and others. My mission is to simplify simulation and computational learning for researchers, especially those just starting their journey.

My work involves both theory and practical simulations — connecting students with real-world projects and helping them master the use of Material Studio and related platforms.
