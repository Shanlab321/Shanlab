About Me

Founder of SHAN Academy of Quantum Simulation & Materials

Zeshan Ahmed

My name is Zeshan Ahmed, and I am an MPhil scholar in Physics with a specialization in materials science. My passion lies in quantum simulations and computational research.

I work with advanced simulation tools to explore the electronic, optical, thermal, and adsorption properties of functional materials. My main focus is on Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations.

To help students and researchers learn these tools, I founded SHAN Academy of Quantum Simulation & Materials (SAQSM) — a virtual platform where I offer hands-on training in using Materials Studio, including modules like CASTEP, DMol³, Forcite, and more.

I aim to simplify complex simulations and make them accessible, especially to those who are just beginning or who don’t have access to advanced labs. I believe that anyone, anywhere, should be able to learn real-world computational skills and apply them in meaningful research.
