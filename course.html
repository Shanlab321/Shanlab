DFT & MD Simulation Course

Practical, hands-on training with real-world simulation tools

Master DFT & MD with Real Projects

This course is designed for beginners and early researchers who want to develop practical skills in Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Molecular Dynamics (MD) using the powerful Materials Studio platform.

Whether you're an undergraduate, master's, or PhD student, this course will guide you step-by-step in building, simulating, and analyzing materials computationally using real-world examples.

What You Will Learn

  • Fundamental concepts of DFT and MD
  • Structure optimization and geometry setup
  • Band structure, DOS, and optical property simulations
  • Surface and adsorption studies
  • Charge analysis (Mulliken, Hirshfeld, ELF)
  • Thermal and mechanical stability using MD
  • Data visualization and publication-ready results

Modules You Will Use

  • CASTEP
  • DMol³
  • Forcite
  • GULP
  • Adsorption Locator
  • Reflex
  • ONETEP
  • Amorphous Cell
  • Polymer Builder
  • Crystal Builder
  • CASTEP Geometry Optimization
  • VESTA, Origin

Course Details

  • Duration: 4–6 weeks
  • Mode: Online (YouTube, Zoom, Google Meet, WhatsApp)
  • Language: Urdu and English
  • Certificate: Provided after completion
  • Support: 1-on-1 guidance and help with your project
