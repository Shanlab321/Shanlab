DFT & MD Simulation Course

SHAN Academy of Quantum Simulation & Materials

Master DFT & MD with Real Projects

This course is designed for beginners and early researchers who want to develop practical skills in Density Functional Theory (DFT) and Molecular Dynamics (MD) using the powerful Materials Studio platform.

Whether you're an undergraduate, master's, or PhD student, this course will guide you step-by-step in building, simulating, and analyzing materials computationally using real-world examples.

What You Will Learn

  • Fundamental concepts of DFT and MD
  • Structure optimization and geometry setup
  • Band structure, DOS, and optical property simulations
  • Adsorption and surface interaction studies
  • Charge analysis (Mulliken, Hirshfeld, ELF)
  • Molecular dynamics simulations for stability and thermal behavior
  • Result interpretation, plotting, and publishing-quality outputs

Modules You Will Use

  • CASTEP – Band structure, DOS, and optical property calculations
  • DMol³ – Adsorption, surface chemistry, charge analysis
  • Forcite – Molecular dynamics, geometry optimization, thermal analysis
  • GULP – Lattice dynamics and force-field-based simulations
  • Adsorption Locator – Predicting adsorption sites on surfaces
  • Reflex – X-ray diffraction (XRD) simulations
  • ONETEP – Large-scale DFT (advanced)
  • Amorphous Cell – Amorphous material and liquid modeling
  • Polymer Builder – Designing and simulating polymers
  • CASTEP Geometry Optimization – Structure relaxation
  • Crystal Builder – Creating crystal structures
  • Visualization – Using VESTA, Origin, and built-in tools
